Undervalued Sector Ranking: Method Detail

This page shows the ranking criteria used before naming sector candidates.

Ranking Criteria

CriterionHow It Was UsedWhy It Matters
Valuation contextSPDR sector fact sheets and broad market context were used where available.A low price move alone is not enough; the sector must have plausible valuation support.
Price actionYahoo chart data through 2026-05-15 showed which sectors were already hot or cold.Cold sectors can be cheap, but also can be value traps.
Business durabilityDemand cyclicality, rate sensitivity, credit sensitivity, and policy risk were scored qualitatively.Cheap sectors with weak survivability do not qualify as attractive.
Crash behaviorPreference went to areas that can hold up operationally in a downturn.The user asked for undervaluation and safer bets if correction happens.
