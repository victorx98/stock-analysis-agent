Interpretive Chain

The correction case does not depend on one forecast. It depends on crowded expectations meeting a market that is already sensitive to rates, fiscal risk, and credit quality. If AI capex remains profitable and rates stay contained, a correction can be shallow. If AI capex disappoints while long rates rise, the selloff can become self-reinforcing.

What Would Lower The Risk Estimate

Broader market participation, falling long-term real yields, lower credit spreads, and evidence that AI capex is producing high returns would reduce the probability range. A correction in AI leaders without credit stress would also reduce crash risk by deflating excess gradually.
