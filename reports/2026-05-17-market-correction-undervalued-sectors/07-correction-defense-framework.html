Safer Bets In A Correction: Framework Detail

This page explains why the main report prefers liquidity and quality over blanket "defensive stock" labels.

Why T-Bills Ranked First

T-bills are not presented as high-return assets. They ranked first because they preserve optionality and avoid duration risk. In a forced-selling event, the ability to buy later can be more valuable than trying to guess the exact defensive equity winner today.
