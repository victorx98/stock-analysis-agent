# News and Catalyst Analysis Rules

## Catalyst categories

Classify news into:

- earnings and guidance
- product launch or roadmap
- regulatory or legal
- macro or rates
- management and governance
- M&A or strategic partnership
- competition and market share
- supply chain
- customer demand
- capital allocation
- analyst or market sentiment

## Materiality test

A news item is material only if it plausibly affects one or more of:

- revenue growth
- margins
- free cash flow
- balance sheet risk
- valuation multiple
- competitive position
- regulatory/legal risk
- investor sentiment over the chosen horizon

## Trust rules

- Use company or regulator sources for confirmation when possible.
- Treat news reports as leads until corroborated.
- Do not rely on headlines alone.
- Separate confirmed developments from speculation.
- Note stale news that is no longer relevant.

## Output expectations

Update `news-digest.md` with:

1. Top material news items.
2. Source tier and URL/file path.
3. Bullish interpretation.
4. Bearish interpretation.
5. Expected time horizon.
6. Follow-up questions.
